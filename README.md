Anru3.6-35B is a transgender-friendly 35B-parameter large language model built upon our research group’s self-developed NightCruise base model and converted into the GGUF format for local inference and deployment.

This model is designed for transgender people and their supporters, aiming to provide a more respectful, inclusive, and safer conversational experience in both Chinese and English. During training and optimization, we used 31,500 authorized transgender-friendly psychological support dialogues and daily supportive chat records. These materials were jointly reviewed by three transgender individuals, two volunteers with more than five years of experience in transgender support work, and two mental health counselors with more than five years of counseling experience who explicitly support transgender people. This project aims to provide transgender people with an AI dialogue tool that is more understanding, inclusive, and emotionally supportive.
